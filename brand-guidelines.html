Brand Personalities

Accessible

An Accessible brand personality values openness and is readily available to targeted customers. Lowering barriers and creating access, space, and transparency are essential elements. Visually, this can be expressed in designs inviting participation, like fonts that mimic handwriting or communicate in a friendly tone.
